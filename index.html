HangMan Is a simple word game in which a player tries to guess a secret word by guessing its letters one by one. For every incorrect guess, a part of a human body is drawn on a board - an arm, a leg, a head, etc. - until the entire body is drawn. The objective of the game is to guess the secret word before the entire body is drawn. The game is sometimes used as an educational game to encourage the development of spelling and vocabulary skills.
